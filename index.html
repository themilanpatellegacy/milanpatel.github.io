Milan Patel — Writer

Thinking out loud about science and society

I'm Milan Patel — a writer fascinated by how scientific and technological shifts ripple through culture, education, and daily life.

My work challenges comfortable assumptions. I believe the most important questions are often the ones we're afraid to ask out loud — and that clear writing is an act of respect toward the reader.

My latest essay, The Last Classroom, examines how artificial intelligence is exposing a two-century-old flaw in the way we educate the next generation.
